Our Hour of Code Event!

We as ADA university students organized our "Hour of Code" event, which is dedicated to Computer Science week, on 14th November at Baku Secondary School-Lyceum No.83 with 7th grade students. Firstly we had informative section of our event about ADA university, then we informed students about programming and Code.org website. We were glad to see how well students did all the given Code.org's Classic Maze tasks and actively participated during the event.
